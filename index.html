x-proto is an online platform that aims to build and support a decentralized community of artists and like-minded people. We seek to amplify art and ideas that do not have an immediate place in our contemporary creative economy. We want this platform to foster connectivity and empathy across a global network.

The initial form of this project concentrates on live streaming. Adapting to new forms of broadcasting and digital spaces is important to our mission and we hope to grow into other technologies in the near future.

To inquire about possible collaborations, showcases, contributions, and more, please email us at xprotostream@gmail.com, or find us on instagram @x_proto.club.
